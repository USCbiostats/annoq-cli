Commands

annoq load
  -c [config file]
  –vcf [vcf file][optional]
  –rsid [rsid list file][optional]
  –chr [chromosome][optional]

annoq search 
  -s [search type]
  –chr [chromosome][optional]
  –start [position start]
  –end [position end]
  –gp [gene product]

annoq search_chr
  –chr [chromosome][optional]
  –start [position start]
  –end [position end]

annoq search_gp
  –gp [gene product]

annoq search_rsid

annoq reset

annoq download
  -o [output]
  –type [Jason | csv | tsv ]

annoq submit (for bigger jobs)
